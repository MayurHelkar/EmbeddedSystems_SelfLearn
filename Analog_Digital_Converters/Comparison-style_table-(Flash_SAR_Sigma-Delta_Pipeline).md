
| **Parameter**            | **Flash ADC**                                                               | **SAR ADC**                                                     | **Sigma-Delta (ΔΣ) ADC**                         | **Pipeline ADC**                                   |
| ------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Basic Principle**      | Parallel comparators compare input with all reference levels simultaneously | Successive approximation using binary search (DAC + comparator) | Oversampling + noise shaping + digital filtering | Multi-stage conversion in cascaded pipeline stages |
| **Speed**                | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Extremely fast (GSPS range)                                           | ⭐⭐⭐ Medium (MSPS range)                                         | ⭐ Low to medium (kSPS to MSPS)                   | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ High (tens to hundreds of MSPS)               |
| **Resolution**           | ⭐ Low–Medium (4–8 bits typical)                                             | ⭐⭐⭐ Medium–High (8–18 bits)                                     | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Very high (16–24+ bits)                    | ⭐⭐⭐ Medium–High (8–16 bits)                        |
| **Power Consumption**    | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Very high                                                             | ⭐⭐ Low                                                          | ⭐⭐⭐ Low–Medium                                   | ⭐⭐⭐ Medium–High                                    |
| **Complexity**           | Very high comparator count (2ⁿ − 1)                                         | Moderate digital + analog balance                               | High digital filtering complexity                | High analog + digital calibration complexity       |
| **Latency**              | Near-zero                                                                   | Low                                                             | High (due to filtering)                          | Medium (pipeline delay)                            |
| **Accuracy**             | Limited by comparator mismatch                                              | Good                                                            | Excellent (noise shaping improves precision)     | Good, but needs calibration                        |
| **Area (Chip Size)**     | Very large                                                                  | Small                                                           | Medium                                           | Medium–Large                                       |
| **Aliasing Sensitivity** | High (needs strong input filtering)                                         | Moderate                                                        | Very low (oversampling helps)                    | Moderate                                           |
| **Noise Performance**    | High noise floor                                                            | Moderate                                                        | Very low noise (best SNR)                        | Moderate                                           |
| **Best For**             | Radar, oscilloscopes, ultra-fast signals                                    | Embedded systems, general-purpose ADCs                          | Audio, precision measurement, instrumentation    | Communications, video, high-speed data acquisition |
| **Key Advantage**        | Fastest possible conversion                                                 | Best balance of power, speed, resolution                        | Highest precision and noise suppression          | Good compromise of speed and resolution            |
| **Key Limitation**       | Power + hardware explosion                                                  | Slower than Flash                                               | High latency                                     | Complex design + calibration needs                 |
